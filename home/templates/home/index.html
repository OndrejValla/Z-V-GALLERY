About

Zuzu Valla is Slovak photographer, currently living in the U.K.
Zuzu is defining her very own and recognisable signature of the photography style by exploring multiple layers of distinct human beauty.
She was brought up in a traditional Slovak family, growing up in the last years of strong communism regime and social transformation of Slovakia.
Zuzuâ€™s grandfather was a keen amateur photographer shooting in black and white, while her grandmother had been using special painting method to colour his photographs.
Zuzu started her interest in photography almost by accident in her late 20s. After being disappointed by using a mobile phone camera, she reached for her first DSLR camera and has never looked back. Photography had become her passion.
Zuzu is excited about capturing the moments where beauty, sadness and mystery come together, creating her unique visual signature.
